Rules

Verb To Be (+ / -)

Use the Verb To Be (Am / Is / Are) to say who you are, or how you feel.

"I am alive." (I am not dead)

"You are afraid."

"She is a girl."

Rules

Questions

To make a question, invert the verb!

"She is alive?"

"Is she alive?"

"Are you afraid?"

Rules

Wh- Questions

WHAT (O que / Qual)

"What are you afraid of?"

WHERE (Onde)

"Where is the girl?"

WHO (Quem)

"Who are you?"
